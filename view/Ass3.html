What is the difference between ON OAGE and OFF PAGE SEO?

"TThe difference between on page and off page SEO comes down to control. In on page SEO, you can target a search term through keyword research, create a page, and tweak the content for the best results. You have complete control over your optimization efforts. Off Page SEO is different. It relies more on promotion. These include link building, blogging, social media, and other off page SEO techniques to build awareness. Control is in the hands of your community."
